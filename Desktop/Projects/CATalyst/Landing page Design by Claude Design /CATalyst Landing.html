Q 14 · QA · ARITHMETIC · MEDIUM A shopkeeper marks his goods 40% above cost, then gives a 20% discount. What is his net profit percentage?
YOUR THINKING
40% − 20% = 20% profit
✕ The discount isn't on cost—it's on the marked price

You subtracted 20% from 40% and called it 20% profit. The discount applies to the marked price, not the cost — that's the whole game.

THE REAL MATH
CP × 1.4 × 0.8 = 1.12 × CP
⚡ Successive percentages compound, they don't add

Marked = 1.4 × CP. Selling = 0.8 × Marked = 1.12 × CP. That's exactly 12% profit — not 20.
