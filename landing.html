Q 14 · QA ARITHMETIC MEDIUM
A shopkeeper marks his goods 40% above cost price, then gives a 20% discount. What is his net profit percentage?
⚠ TRAP
You subtracted 20% from 40% to get a 20% profit. The discount is applied on the marked price, not the cost price — that's the entire game.
💡 KEY INSIGHT
Successive percentages don't add or subtract. They compound. Marked = 1.4 × CP. Selling = 0.8 × Marked = 1.12 × CP. Profit = 12%.
✓ HOW TO FIX
Whenever you see "marked up X%, discount Y%", write (1+x)(1−y) immediately. Reason in multipliers, not words.
